Our Startup Story

Raphael InnovaCore Solutions was born from a simple but powerful idea — that young Africans have the creativity, determination, and intelligence to build world-class businesses. Founded by Raphael Ramashamole in Maseru, Lesotho, the company started as a small consulting practice helping local entrepreneurs navigate the challenges of starting and growing a business.

What began as a one-person operation quickly grew into a dynamic team of thinkers, builders, and doers. Today, Raphael InnovaCore Solutions serves clients across Southern Africa, offering business consulting, digital solutions, and market expansion services.

Our journey has not been without challenges, but every obstacle has made us stronger, smarter, and more committed to our mission of empowering entrepreneurs across the continent.

Meet the Founder

The person behind the vision of Raphael InnovaCore Solutions.

Raphael Ramashamole
Founder & CEO

Raphael Ramashamole is a passionate entrepreneur with a background in Bachelor of Business in Entrepreneurship and information technology. He founded Raphael InnovaCore Solutions with the goal of making quality business support accessible to young entrepreneurs in Lesotho and across Africa.

With over five years of experience in business development and digital strategy, Raphael leads the company with a hands-on approach, always putting clients and community first.
