Our Business Model

How Raphael InnovaCore Solutions creates value and generates revenue.

How We Operate

Raphael InnovaCore Solutions operates on a service-based business model, generating revenue by providing high-quality business consulting, digital solutions, and training programs to clients across Lesotho and Southern Africa.

We serve a wide range of clients — from individual entrepreneurs just starting out, to established small and medium enterprises looking to grow and innovate. Our tiered pricing model ensures that businesses at every stage can access our services.

We also generate income through training workshops, online courses, and strategic partnerships with organisations that share our vision of empowering African entrepreneurs.

Revenue Streams

The main ways Raphael InnovaCore Solutions earns income.

💲

Service Fees

Clients pay for consulting sessions, digital development projects, and marketing campaigns on a project or monthly retainer basis.

🏫

Training Programs

We run paid entrepreneurship workshops and bootcamps for groups of aspiring business owners, generating event-based income.

🤝

Partnership Commissions

We earn referral commissions by connecting clients with our trusted network of suppliers, financiers, and service providers.

How We Work With Clients

Our simple and transparent process from first contact to delivery.

  1. Step 1 — Free Consultation: The client contacts us and we schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to understand their needs and goals.
  2. Step 2 — Proposal: We prepare a custom proposal outlining the scope of work, timeline, and pricing for the client's review.
  3. Step 3 — Agreement: Once the client approves the proposal, we sign a simple service agreement and collect a deposit to begin work.
  4. Step 4 — Execution: Our team carries out the project with regular check-ins and progress updates to keep the client informed.
  5. Step 5 — Delivery: We deliver the final product or report, walk the client through the results, and collect the final payment.
  6. Step 6 — Support: We provide 30 days of after-delivery support to ensure the client is fully satisfied and equipped to move forward.
